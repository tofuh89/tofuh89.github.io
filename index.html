Teaching Liberation

Dr. Agnes Trzak is an Activist-Scholar interested in queer feminist antispeciesism. Her most recent book Teaching Liberation is available now from Lantern Books.

When she isn't writing, giving talks or workshops, she is either organising with the Anti-Speciesist Collective or working as a counsellor with women, queer people and vegans.

Her recent chapter on disability and the ahuman appears in Crip Perspectives in Critical Animal Studies. Her doctoral thesis concerning the dismantling of Kyriarchy can also be found below.
